Our Founders

Bryan Smith - CEO

Bryan Smith is the chief executive officer of Rave. He spent four years building Rave through the love of music. Bryan is responsible for setting the strategic direction of the company, driving growth, and ensuring that Rave continues to innovate and deliver unparalleled value to users worldwide.

Michael Rodriguez - CTO

Michael Rodriguez brings a wealth of technical expertise to his role as Chief Technology Officer at Rave Music. With a background in software engineering and a passion for innovation, Michael oversees the development and implementation of cutting-edge technology solutions that power the Rave platform and deliver an exceptional user experience.

David Lee - CFO

David Lee is a finance and operations expert with extensive experience in scaling high-growth startups. As Chief Financial Officer of Rave Music, David oversees all financial operations, including budgeting, forecasting, and financial reporting, ensuring the long-term financial health and sustainability of the company.
